You know what the fun part of web development is? The logic. The behind-the-scenes. The stuff you don’t notice until something’s going wrong. That’s where the excitement’s at, and that’s where I prefer to spend my time.

That being said, while I’m not nearly as interested in how something looks than in how it works, I realize that the two concepts are very intertwined. Good design works better than bad design. And while I don’t consider myself a designer, I definitely know whether or not I like a given design. In any case, I’ve gotten to know my way around Bootstrap pretty well by now, so I can definitely make something functional and attractive.

Hope you enjoy what I’ve built, and let me know if you have any questions!
